Dictionary app

requirements: Words, their meaning, part of speech, and example sentences are stored in a file. Words can be noun, verb or adjective. Read words from the file and display words with their meaning(printMeaning() method should print the meaning) to the user.

If the word is,
	a) a noun plural form of that word will also contain in the meaning
	b) a verb an example sentence should be in the meaning
	c) a adjective synonyms are mentioned in the meaning

implement: inheritance, package, and file operations
